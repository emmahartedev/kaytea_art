About me

Hello

I'm Katie, welcome to my website.

Living in Clonakilty, West Cork. I've worked for the past 9 years as a Veterinary Nurse. Ever since I was little, I've adored animals. Chickens, dogs, ferrets.. even wild birds. You name it, we've probably shared a roof at least once in this lifetime!

I studied art in school, keeping it up as a hobby throughout university and my working career. Art for me has become an obsession. Bringing animals to life on paper is my passion.

Working closely with animals has given me a true appreciation for the individual characters. Personality is exactly what I aim to capture in an art piece.

As well as original artwork, prints and cards, I create custom Pet Commissions in my studio. If you have any questions, please reach out. I'd love to hear from you!
